Our Story

How we met?

In September 2020, we both arrived at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign for our Masters.

We were among the very few to be able to make it to the US in person during COVID. We connected online before we reached and were the first people we met here, in front of a supermarket on campus, County Market.

Friends to much more!

We soon became great friends and started spending a lot of time together. We'd go for walks, cook food together, go around exploring campus!

Soon, we fell in love!

We went on many trips, hiking in national parks, exploring cities, to beaches!

Meeting each others' families

Since we were in the US, we met and got to know each others' families on video calls. Our parents visited and spent time with each other, and we'd join them on Zoom.

Proposal

We had talked about getting married one day. In November 2022, Pranav asked Shivani if she'd spend the rest of her life with him, and she said yes!

Engagement

With our families' blessings, we got engaged to each other in March 2023 in Pune.

Fun fact: We met each other's families in person for the first time during the engagement.

We're really looking forward to our wedding and our life together.

We can't wait to share our big day with you!
